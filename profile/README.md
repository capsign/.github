## Company Mission

Our mission is to enable more opportunities and greater economic benefits for private companies, shareholders, and investors through blockchain technology. We believe that interoperability will be a major driver for compliance automation, deep liquidity, and composability between financial services. We're focused specifically on private markets by improving workflows for transactions involving restricted securities.

## Features and Capabilities

### Securities Tokenization

Our platform supports the tokenization of the following assets:

- Common Stock
- Preferred Stock
- SAFE Contracts
- Stock Options
- Convertible Notes
- Warrants

### Securities Interoperability

We are working on a variety of equity tokenization use cases:

- Automatic synchronization with equity management platforms
- Stock-option exercise and financings using tokenized cash
- Encumbering restricted securities for collateralized loans
- Early liquidity through on-chain private secondary sales
- Fundraising for early-stage ventures with tokenized SAFEs
- Real-time tax liability estimation and automated reporting
- Integration with accounting software for on-chain reporting

## Legal & Compliance

CapSign is committed to compliance with applicable laws and regulations in the jurisdictions where our platform operates. We are developing robust programs for compliance with anti-money laundering and securities laws. We also are committed to the highest ethical standards for data protection and compliance with jurisdictional data protection laws.
